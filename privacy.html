WordSwipe Privacy Policy

WordSwipe Privacy Policy Effective Date: March 16, 2025 Last Updated: March 2025 1. Introduction Welcome to WordSwipe (the “App”). WordSwipe is a mobile vocabulary learning application that helps users learn English words through swipe-based flashcards, spaced repetition, daily goals, and push notification reminders. This Privacy Policy explains how WordSwipe handles information when you use our App. We are committed to protecting your privacy. Please read this policy carefully. By downloading or using WordSwipe, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App. 2. Information We Collect 2.1 Information You Provide WordSwipe does not require you to create an account or log in. We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number. 2.2 Information Stored Locally on Your Device WordSwipe stores the following data locally on your device only: Your learning progress (learned words, difficult words, quiz results) Daily learning streak and experience points (XP) App preferences and settings (e.g., dark mode, daily goal size) This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers. 2.3 Information Collected Automatically The App itself does not automatically collect usage analytics or crash reports. However, certain third-party services integrated into the App may collect limited technical data as described below. 3. Advertising (Google AdMob) Free users of WordSwipe may see advertisements served by Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service provided by Google LLC. 3.1 Data Collected by AdMob Google AdMob may collect and use the following types of data for advertising purposes: Device advertising identifiers (e.g., IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android) IP address and approximate geographic location Device type, operating system version, and app version Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks) 3.2 How to Limit Ad Tracking You can limit ad tracking or reset your advertising identifier at any time: iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable “Allow Apps to Request to Track” Android: Settings → Google → Ads → “Delete advertising ID” or opt out of personalized ads For more information about how Google uses data for advertising, please visit: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads 3.3 Premium Subscription — No Ads Users who purchase a premium subscription will not be shown any advertisements. AdMob will not collect advertising-related data for premium users within the App. 4. Push Notifications WordSwipe may send you push notifications as daily reminders to complete your learning goals. Notifications are optional and require your explicit permission. We request notification permission only once; you can grant or deny it at any time. You can disable notifications at any time via your device’s system Settings. Push notification functionality is powered by Expo Notifications. Expo may store a push token associated with your device to deliver notifications. This token is a technical identifier and is not linked to any personal identity. For more information about Expo’s data practices, see https://expo.dev/privacy. 5. Third-Party Services WordSwipe integrates the following third-party services. Each service has its own privacy policy: Google AdMob Purpose: Serving advertisements to free users Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy Expo (by Expo, Inc.) Purpose: App build infrastructure and push notification delivery Privacy Policy: https://expo.dev/privacy Free Dictionary API (dictionaryapi.dev) Purpose: Optionally fetching word definitions, phonetics, and usage examples Data sent: Only the English word being looked up (no personal data) Results are cached locally on your device after the first request Service info: https://dictionaryapi.dev No personal data is transmitted to dictionaryapi.dev. 6. Data Storage and Security 6.1 Local Storage All learning progress data (words learned, quiz results, streaks, settings) is stored exclusively on your device using AsyncStorage. This data is not uploaded to any server and remains under your control. 6.2 No Cloud Sync WordSwipe does not currently offer cloud backup or sync. If you uninstall the App, your local learning progress will be permanently deleted. 6.3 Security Although all data is stored locally, we recommend keeping your device software up to date to benefit from the latest security patches provided by your operating system. 7. Children’s Privacy WordSwipe is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under the age of 13 has provided personal information through the App, please contact us at anilgoktepe07@gmail.com and we will take steps to remove such information. If you are between 13 and 17 years of age, please review this policy with a parent or guardian before using the App. 8. Your Rights Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data: Access: You can view all of your learning data directly within the App. Deletion: You can reset all progress data at any time via Settings → Reset Progress. Uninstalling the App also deletes all locally stored data. Opt-out of personalized ads: You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time using your device’s settings (see Section 3.2). Notification control: You can enable or disable push notifications at any time via your device’s system settings. Since WordSwipe does not collect personal data on its own servers, there is no account or server-side profile for us to retrieve or delete on your behalf. 9. Policy Updates We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App, third-party services, or applicable law. When we make changes, we will: Update the “Effective Date” at the top of this policy Post the updated policy on our website and/or within the App We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Your continued use of WordSwipe after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. 10. Contact Information If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us: Email: support@wordswipe.app App: WordSwipe Platform: iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play) We will do our best to respond to all inquiries within a reasonable timeframe. This Privacy Policy is compliant with App Store Review Guidelines and Google Play Developer Program Policies.
